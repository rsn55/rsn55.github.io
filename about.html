About Me

I am a third year computer science student at Cornell University with an interest in front-end web development. At school, I work with Cornell Design and Tech Initiative to create technology for community impact. My current project for the team involves creating an online queue to make office hours more efficient and helpful. I am a member of Cornell Concert Commission, which selects bands, promotes shows, and produces all other aspects of popular concerts at Cornell. I am also a teaching assistant for Operating Systems (and former TA of Systems Organization and Programming). In my free time, I listen to music, attend concerts, dance, draw, and paint.
