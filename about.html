About Me

I am a third year Computer Science student at Cornell University with a minor in Lingusitics. I am especially interested in full-stack web development. This past summer, I was a software engineering intern on the data security team at Cisco Systems.

At Cornell, I am a teaching assistant for Operating Systems (and former teaching assistant for Systems Organization and Programming). I am also a developer for Cornell Design and Tech Initiative, an engineering project team that creates technology to help Cornell students and the surrounding community.

I love music and attending live performances, which is why I volunteer for Cornell Concert Commission, a club that selects bands, promotes shows, and produces all aspects of popular concerts at Cornell. In addition, my passion for art inspired me to become a volunteer for Art Beyond Cornell, an organization that provides art lessons to incarcerated youth at a local detention center.
