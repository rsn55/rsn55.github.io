About Me

I am a senior at Cornell University majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Linguistics. This past summer, I was a full-stack software engineering intern at Ab Initio Software and was previously a software engineering intern at Cisco Systems. My primary interest is in front-end development.

At Cornell, I am a teaching assistant for Functional Programming (and former teaching assistant for Operating Systems and the Systems Organization and Programming course). As a TA, I co-lead a recitation section of 40 students twice a week, grade assignments, and create review material for tests. I am also a developer for Cornell Design and Tech Initiative, an engineering project team that creates technology to help Cornell students and the surrounding community.

I love music and attending live performances, which is why I volunteer for Cornell Concert Commission, a club that selects bands, promotes shows, and produces all aspects of popular concerts at Cornell. I am also a brother of Phi Sigma Pi, the National Honor Fraternity.
