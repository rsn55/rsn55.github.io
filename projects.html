Internal Project Management App

I implemented the front-end features for this flask app created from scratch with a team of two other interns.
The site was created to cut costs ($50,000 annually) by reducing Salesforce use.
It allows managers to track their resources, visualize their team's time utilization, and easily assign tasks.
Some of my contributions include a view of all ongoing projects that can be sorted and filtered, graphs displaying employee time utilization, and a tool for adding and editing tasks.

Research Connect

I have been a front-end designer for this project for over a year, using React to create a website that allows undergraduates seeking research opportunities to find and apply to labs.
A couple of my contributions include a filter and search page and a feature that compares student information and position requirements to determine good matches.
Research Connect is a sub-team of Cornell Design and Tech Initiative.

Game Design: Headspin

I, along with 5 other programmers and designers, created Headspin from scratch for Intro to Game Design. This puzzle-solving desktop platformer was made using LibGDX, a Java-based engine. My role was primarily to produce original artwork for the project and to assist in writing our manual and design documentation. It was displayed in the annual GDIAC showcase.
